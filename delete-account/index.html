Account & Data Deletion

This page provides instructions on how to request the deletion of your Melos account and all associated data, in compliance with app store policies and data privacy regulations.

How to Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your account at any time directly from within the Melos app. This is the fastest and most secure way to remove your data.

  1. Open the Melos app and ensure you are logged in to the account you wish to delete.
  2. Navigate to the Profile tab (the person icon in the bottom menu).
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the Profile screen.
  4. Tap the "Delete Account" button.
  5. Read the confirmation message carefully and confirm your decision to permanently delete your account.

Warning: Deleting your account is a permanent and irreversible action. Once confirmed, your account and all associated data cannot be recovered.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

When you confirm the deletion through the in-app process, we immediately and permanently remove all of your personal data from our systems. There is no retention period.

The types of data that will be deleted include:

  • Account Credentials: Your email address and any links to your Google or Apple accounts used for authentication.
  • Profile Information: Your username, onboarding answers (language proficiency, music preferences, age range, learning goals), and any other profile details.
  • Learning Progress: All of your quiz scores, song progress, and completion history.
  • Library Data: All of your liked songs and listening history.
  • Submitted Content: Any feedback you have sent through the app.

Need Help?

If you are unable to access your account or the in-app deletion feature for any reason, please contact our support team for assistance with your deletion request.
