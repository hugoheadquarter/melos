About Melos

Melos is a mobile application that transforms how people learn languages by combining interactive lyrics, detailed breakdowns, and progress tracking with their favorite music.

Our platform enables users to learn new languages through synchronized lyrics, comprehensive vocabulary explanations, and engaging quizzes—all while enjoying the music they love.

With a global community spanning over 140 countries and a strong social media presence, Melos is redefining language education for the modern learner.

Platform Features

Interactive Lyrics

Synchronized bilingual lyrics with tap-to-jump navigation for seamless learning.

Detailed Breakdowns

Comprehensive vocabulary, grammar explanations, and contextual examples for every line.

Progress Tracking

Quiz-based knowledge testing and personalized libraries to track learning journey.
